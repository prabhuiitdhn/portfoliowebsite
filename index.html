I am a Technical Specialist in Machine Learning at Continental Automotive, Bengaluru, specializing in computer vision and autonomous vehicle perception. I work at the intersection of research and product — taking ideas from prototype to ASIL-D safety-certified hardware deployment.

My work covers the full AI stack: raw sensor data from IMX728/IMX623 cameras, synthetic data pipelines, large-scale GPU training, model optimization, and deployment on Ambarella CV3-AD 685 SoC with 2ms latency and 3 eTOPS targets.

Beyond work, I actively study LLMs from scratch, efficient deep learning (MIT 6.5940), and Transformers (Stanford CME-295) to stay at the frontier.
